Blackjack

This is standard Blackjack, it uses 6 decks.

Rules

Dealer will hit until 17.
You cannot split the hand in this Blackjack game.

How to: Blackjack

Dealer will lay out two cards in front of you, and one visible and one invisible card in front of himself.
After laying the cards out, you will be asked to "hit" or "stay".
Hitting will give you another card.
Staying will keep your current hand, and it will be the dealer's turn.
